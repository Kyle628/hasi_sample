Natural Color

Häsi is painted with intention—in process, but also ingredients. Our blue and yellow colors come naturally from butterfly pea flowers and turmeric root. And the ingredients themselves come from people, and stories Häsi believes in. These agents of visual creative give purpose to our slogan: Art is in Bread.

Heirloom Single Origin Turmeric Grown on small farms in the Andhra Pradesh region of India, this turmeric contains 2x the curcumin content over store bought brands. Curcumin is the good stuff—the active ingredient—in turmeric: anti-inflammatory, antioxidant and improved brain function are some of the studied health benefits of the spice. Beyond its nutritional value, turmeric renders a warm, yellow tint that reminds us of beautiful California sunshine.

Butterfly Blue Pea Flowers These flowers are what Gregor Mendel (the “father” of modern genetics) used to pioneer the study of how genes are passed down. Pea flowers come in a variety of colors, and because of their strong pigment, Mendel was able to identify color differences in his research.

Sourced from Thailand, Häsi uses the pea flower for natural blue coloring. And studies suggest that they come with their own string of health benefits: antioxidant, anti-anxiety, anti-depression, bioflavonoid, and more. Like with turmeric, butterfly pea flowers exhibit both nutritional benefit as well as captivating aesthetic.
