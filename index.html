Häsi: Art Is In Bread.

Häsi started in 2017, with an experimental fusion between natural color, and traditional Austrian bread. The first loaves were made with superfoods Turmeric, and Blue Pea Flower. These created the original blue and yellow Häsi loaf. Since then, Häsi has expanded, but still only uses the finest, natural ingredients.
